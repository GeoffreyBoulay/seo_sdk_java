Answer: 
Mechanical dryers remove short, loose little fibers as they dry, like the ones you see caught in the lint trap. These little fibers, if not removed, cling to the weave of the fabric, restricting natural stretch and movement. Thus, the clothes feel stiff.

I actually own this GE Profile dryer and have found that I don't even need fabric softener when I use it.
