Answer: 
There are specific credit cards that offer the frequent flier miles on them. What you need to do though is to research multiple banks to see what they offer. How it works is there's a tradeoff between earning miles and having a high interest rate. Ideally you would use these cards, then pay off the balance a soon as you can. That way you earn miles without the high interest payments.

Answer: 
The number of cards doesn't not affect your score. The main factors that do affect it are; your payment history, too many inquires on your credit report and if your credit cards are maxed out. It is always a good idea to keep you credit at about 25% of your limit. What you want is a high credit limit with a low balance, plus a number of years with good credit.
